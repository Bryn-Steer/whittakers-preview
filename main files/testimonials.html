Family-owned and proudly serving Urmston since 1984, Whittaker's Fish & Chip Shop is known for freshly cooked fish & chips, homemade favourites, gluten-free options, generous portions and friendly service. Enjoy a traditional British chippy experience whether you're dining in our café or ordering a takeaway.
